About Surprisewala

A brand built around creating happiness for the people you love most.

Surprisewala began with a simple intention: making loved ones feel seen through thoughtful, emotional surprises. What started as personal birthday planning evolved into a celebration brand known for midnight reveals, romantic concepts and carefully curated setups that turn ordinary moments into unforgettable memories.

The catalog story highlights a launch on 1 September 2021 and a key momentum moment on 24 March 2023, when a memorable midnight car surprise helped define the brand's identity. Today the mission remains clear: spreading happiness anywhere and everywhere possible through beautifully planned surprises.

Mission Spreading happiness and playing the cupid role between giver and receiver.
Vision To become the first brand people remember when they think of surprises.
Style Elegant, emotional, customizable and designed around real reactions.

"Spreading happiness anywhere and everywhere possible in the name of surprises."
